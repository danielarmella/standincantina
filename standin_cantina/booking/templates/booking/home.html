Devon Elora has been a stand-in for almost 20 years in both film and television. She also authored the book "Standing In For Single Camera." While working on Westworld, she created a facebook group for her 2nd Team family in order to find them work. Over time, those stand-ins added their 2nd Team friends to the group and the Stand-in Cantina was formed. Her goal is to help wonderful, professional stand-ins get booked, because they deserve it!

We would love our family to grow, but we are extremely particular in who becomes a member. All are welcome to submit by adding yourself to the facebook group "Stand-in Cantina Booking" By doing that, you will be prompted to answer a series of questions. If those are answered correctly, we will ask our mutual Assistant Director and/or stand-in friends for their recommendations. It's not just a good resume we look for...it's a team player who would be great spending 12 hours a day on set with. We will be having stand-in classes soon! Keep a lookout on our facebook page. :)
